Too Long; Didn't Listen (TL;DL)

Published: Sat 26 June 2021
By Ken, Patricia, Cicely, Jill

In Introduction.

tags: w210 Capstone

Have you ever wanted to get into a podcast episode, but didn’t listen because it was too long? We're working on a podcast summarization tool called TL;DL, which transcribes a podcast's content and provides an easy to read summarization - no ads, no promotions. Reading a condensed summary of details from a podcast episode can give you a sense of what a new show focuses on or help you catch up on hours’ worth of content in minutes. Whenever you are catching up on previous episodes or browsing for something new, TL;DL will give you the synopses of the podcast of your choice and help you make better use of your time.

Unlike previous radio content, which directly broadcast content over the airwaves, today it is now captured, organized and made available for listening at a time of our choosing. Given the now all but endless supply of audio content for individuals to explore, many uses exist for a summarization, which extends beyond just a hook or an overview of the content. Such opportunities arise in the informational, educational, and pure entertainment arenas.
