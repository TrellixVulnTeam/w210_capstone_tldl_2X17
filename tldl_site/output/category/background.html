Survey Results: Identifying Demand

To calibrate the demand as well as the value for the product, the team surveyed podcast users to understand listener traits and needs. Results indicated that not only do listeners follow multiple broadcasts on a weekly basis, a consistent failure to finish trend exists amongst listeners. Overview metrics are listed below:

These listener traits reflect that broadcast length does indeed impact the listening experience.

Value Added Product Niche

Results from this survey indicated that indeed space exists for an effective summarization tool for audio broadcasts. The majority interest points to it providing aggregated talking points and serving as a decision tool for content exploration.
